DESCRIPCIÓN

Sabrina Carpenter is the queen of the coffee tie-in. The Grammy Award-winning singer of 2024’s song of the summer—if not the whole year—Espresso has collaborated on coffee ice cream, a coffee pop-up with Verve, even a coffee perfume. Which, if I write a song with over two billion spins on Spotify and another 377 million on YouTube, you better believe I’m cashing in on even the most loosely-related word associations that I can. And so if you are experiencing a bit of ear wear, from either the song or the tie-ins, I have some bad news for you. Sabrina Carpenter has made a very cool coffee item, a limited-edition vinyl that has coffee in it. As reported by Billboard, the record was made in collaboration with the UK-based Blood Records, who create unique vinyl offerings, most famously the white-power filled remix of Charlie XCX’s Brat. The 12″ release is in honor of the one-year anniversary of Espresso and is limited to a run of just 1,000 pressings. And honestly, it looks kind of amazing.
